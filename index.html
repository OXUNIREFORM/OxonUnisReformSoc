Who We Are

Oxon Universities' Reform is a cross-university student society focused on policy discussion, political engagement, and promoting institutional accountability.

What We Do

We host speaker events, reading groups, debates, and campaigns focused on reform-oriented politics.

Get Involved

Whether you want to attend events, help organise campaigns, or join the committee, we welcome students from all academic backgrounds.
